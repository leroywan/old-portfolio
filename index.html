About Me

Hi, I am Leroy Wan and I love creating things. I built this website as a way to showcase some of my work as well as to keep track of what I have accomplished so far in terms of learning web development. I started programming because I love the feeling of being able to create anything I want with just a few numbers and letters. Other hobbies I have include playing piano and guitar, camping out in the forest, and bicycle touring. I am currently a 3rd year student studying geological engineering at Queen's University and I am pursuing a career in environmental engineering with an emphasis on geophysical modeling. Feel free to stroll around this website and look at some of the things I have made. I am always learning new technology so projects will frequently be updated!
